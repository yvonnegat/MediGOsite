About Us

Medigo Kenya – Revolutionizing Pharmacy Access

MediGo Kenya is a digital platform dedicated to improving patient care by ensuring access to the right medications at the right time. We bridge the gap between pharmacies, suppliers, and patients to create a seamless healthcare experience.

Our Mission

Enhancing patient health outcomes by making it easier for pharmacies to stock essential medications, track inventory efficiently, and provide better treatment support.

Our Vision

A healthcare system where every patient can access the medication they need without delays, and pharmacies are empowered to serve their communities more effectively.

Why Choose MediGo Kenya?

  • Ensuring Medicine Availability – Patients should never struggle to find essential medications.
  • Connecting Pharmacies & Suppliers – A reliable network to prevent stockouts.
  • Improving Treatment & Feedback – Empowering patients with better follow-ups and pharmacy support.
